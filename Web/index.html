ABOUT US

ASEBD ENGINEERING AND TECHNICAL SERVICES was founded on April 12, 2018 at Cabuyao, Laguna.

The Company started with few employees focusing on trading with different companies around CALABARZON.In the third Quarter of 2022 the company ventured with telecom industry on tower retrofitting and tower reinforcement with several telecom towers at Luzon area.

On the same year, the company expands its role on providing quality services in Design & Construction industry for (Civil, Structural, Mechanical, Electrical, Fire Protection, and Sanitary/Plumbing) with well-trained and experienced manpower capable of providing quality products & services.

Today, ASEBD Engineering and Technical Services is a competitive General contractor company with PCAB license, offering a high quality services in accordance with local and international standards to satisfy and exceed the expectations of a client.
